**Central promise frame.** The owned system presents Cicapair as a combined
redness-management proposition: immediate visible color correction plus a wider
care architecture (OBS-002, OBS-003). The sealed evidence does not show whether
that promise is strengthening or weakening. It is visibly exposed to questions
about formula continuity and color/finish experience (OBS-008, OBS-009), while
the absence of a reproducible retailer-review corpus prevents a distributional
read (GAP-003). A verified formula delta, version-linked response corpus, or
material change in the current collection/channel presentation would change
this view.

### 2. Decision-Neutral Boundary

The report covers one company/brand system through observable positioning,
offerings and claims, markets and channels, dated operating motion, attributable
customer/community examples, one bounded comparator pointer, contradictions,
and gaps. It identifies tensions and the later questions they could inform. It
does not assign pain, cause, severity, priority, demand, prevalence, buyer, ICP,
urgency, willingness to pay, recommendation, response, outreach, offer, or
wedge. Deep competitor treatment requires a separately named follow-up.
