## Status And Decision

`OWNER_LOCKED_PRODUCT_DIRECTION` — adopted by the owner in-thread on
2026-07-14.

Forseti will build toward a **decision-adjudication product**: it helps a
company make a consequential decision by discovering, preserving, structuring,
reconciling, and judging the evidence that can materially change that decision.
Beauty is the first application. The initial product form is a bounded
**Decision Sprint** around one decision and its deadline.

This is horizontal by contract, not universal by assertion. Forseti may serve
different decision families only when each passes the admission contract below.
"We can use it for anything" is therefore an expansion hypothesis, not current
product scope.

The exact beauty buyer, first admitted decision family, commercial offer,
pricing, market sizing, competitor position, and distribution route remain for
the next GTM lane. Candidate beauty decisions in this record are non-ranked
admission examples, not a buyer or wedge decision.

## Buyer Outcome

The buyer does not buy source volume, a research engine, provenance, Cleaning,
or a dashboard. The buyer buys a better-defended action:

```text
decision frame and deadline
-> evidence world and decisive unknowns
-> admissible evidence and explicit gaps
-> reconciled claims, conflicts, dependence, and timing
-> right-sized action, uncertainty, and reversal conditions
-> preserved decision trace
-> later reveal or outcome
-> improved judgment for the next comparable decision
```

The useful output is a decision outcome: act, probe, sequence, delay, hold,
avoid, or another decision-specific action at the strongest defensible ceiling.
Forseti must refuse to force an action when a decisive input is missing.

## Decision Admission Contract

A decision belongs in the product only when all of these are true:

1. A named owner is accountable for a real action by a real deadline.
2. Error or delay has meaningful economic, strategic, or risk consequence.
3. Several differently positioned, timed, incomplete, or strategically biased
   evidence families can materially change the action or confidence.
4. Forseti can lawfully obtain enough decision-relevant evidence to produce a
   credible initial judgment.
5. A strong human analyst can deliver useful judgment now; autonomous Judgment
   is not a prerequisite.
6. The recommendation, action ceiling, uncertainty, and reversal conditions can
   be made inspectable.
7. The paid decision has a faithful proof route: the same decision can be tested
   before purchase or through a blind cutoff/reveal protocol.
8. A later action, reveal, or outcome can improve the decision trace or signal
   reliability, even when outcome resolution is slow or imperfect.
9. Repetition can standardize the evidence model, decision trace, and workflow
   rather than leave the business permanently bespoke.

Admission ends in exactly one current evidence state:

- `ADMIT_PUBLIC_OR_PURCHASED`: legally available public or purchased/entitled
  evidence is sufficient for the decision artifact.
- `ADMIT_WITH_AUTHORIZED_PRIVATE_CONTEXT`: the external baseline is useful, but
  a bounded item of customer-authorized context is required before the action
  can be production-grade.
- `HOLD_DECISIVE_INPUT_MISSING`: an unavailable or unverified input controls the
  action; Forseti returns the gap and does not invent a recommendation.

Representative beauty decisions that may be tested under this contract include
concept or portfolio go/no-go, positioning or claims, channel or retail entry,
launch intervention, competitive response, price or promotion, creator or
marketing allocation, and reputation or safety response. They carry no rank or
inherited commercial priority here.

## Evidence Doctrine: Decision-Relevant Breadth

More **independent, decision-relevant** evidence can produce a better decision.
More rows, mentions, or repeated copies do not necessarily do so. Evidence
breadth earns product value when it changes a load-bearing claim, reveals a
conflict, breaks apparent independence, changes the action ceiling, or reduces
a decisive unknown. Amplified repetition can make a decision worse if counted
as corroboration.

The relevant evidence world may include retailer assortment, price, promotion,
availability and reviews; consumer and specialist communities; creator content,
sponsorship and audience response; search and answer-engine behavior; brand
claims, formulations, launches and archives; advertising and affiliate
activity; hiring and organizational signals; trademarks, patents, regulation,
litigation and filings; distributor, supplier and manufacturing evidence;
marketplace, resale and inventory evidence; and authorized private context or
outcomes. These are evidence families, not separate products and not a required
checklist for every decision.

Forseti may pursue public information and may buy lawful access to entitled or
paywalled material. This is an eligibility posture, not a claim that every
source is already captured, licensed for every use, complete, fresh, economical,
or reachable through a current production route.

Source completeness is always relative to a declared, finite evidence world.
The Capture/Silver lane may support bounded coverage receipts for such a world;
it does not currently prove universal source-world completeness. Sellability of
the Decision Sprint does not depend on that terminal guarantee.

## What Forseti Can Deliver Now

Subject to source-specific access and rights, Forseti can now deliver a manual,
analyst-led Decision Sprint using its current research, capture, preservation,
evidence-structuring, reconciliation, and product-artifact disciplines. A needed
public source may justify building or adapting a route; route-build intent is
not proof of existing standing capture or production-scale monitoring.

A strong analyst can now:

- frame the decision, decisive claims, options, constraints, deadline, and
  evidence cutoff;
- discover and acquire decision-relevant public or purchased evidence through
  authorized routes;
- preserve provenance, timing, missingness, contradiction, and source limits;
- deduplicate obvious repetition, distinguish independence from amplification,
  normalize entities where current tooling or bounded manual work supports it,
  and keep unresolved conflicts visible;
- weigh evidence qualitatively for the claim and decision;
- issue a recommendation, action ceiling, counter-case, uncertainty, reversal
  conditions, and reveal plan.

Current Forseti does **not** claim mature broad-volume Cleaning, autonomous
Judgment, calibrated superiority to strong analysts, production-scale broad
monitoring, or guaranteed source completeness.

## Initial Product Form: Decision Sprint

The Decision Sprint is fixed-scope and deadline-bound around one admitted
decision. Its minimum buyer-facing package is:

1. a decision frame that locks owner, action, options, deadline, constraints,
   evidence cutoff, and privileged-context boundary;
2. an evidence-world and gap note showing what was sought, obtained, unavailable,
   duplicated, dependent, conflicting, or decision-irrelevant;
3. a concise decision memorandum stating the recommended action and action
   ceiling, alternatives, counter-case, uncertainty, and reversal conditions;
4. an inspectable evidence appendix and decision trace;
5. a reveal/outcome plan stating what later evidence would test the judgment.

The Sprint is not consulting by the hour. Scope is controlled by one Decision
Frame, an evidence cutoff, and one decision artifact. The buyer may receive a
deck derived from the memo, but a deck, report, data feed, alert stream, or
dashboard is not the product center.

## Product-Form Progression

The next product form is a recurring **Decision Desk** only after repeated
admitted decisions show a stable workflow, recurring buyer cadence, reusable
evidence structures, observable outcomes, and bounded analyst labor. The Desk
would maintain an upcoming decision book, longitudinal evidence, and
decision-triggered adjudication; it is not a standing dashboard by default.

A software system or platform is later still. It is earned when repeated traces
identify stable interfaces worth automating and when automation reduces labor,
improves consistency, or increases decision throughput without hiding missing
evidence. This progression is a hypothesis, not a roadmap or implementation
authorization.

## Same-Decision Proof Contract

Proof must test the decision the paid Sprint would change, not a neighboring
research task:

- lock the Decision Frame and evidence cutoff before analysis;
- reconstruct only evidence genuinely available by the cutoff;
- seal the Decision Sprint artifact before any later reveal;
- reveal the actual action or outcome only after sealing;
- compare action quality, right-sizing, reasoning, decisive misses, and what the
  buyer could actually have known;
- state which private constraints and production-transfer questions remain
  untested.

Historical cases can prove disciplined same-decision reasoning and expose
failure modes. They do not prove buyer pull, WTP, transfer to a live company,
or superior forecasting. The smallest later pull signal is budget- or
decision-adjacent behavior around a real Decision Frame, not praise for the
research.

## Learning And Defensibility

The compounding asset is not source access, provenance, generic AI, forecasting,
or Cleaning alone. It is the linked history of:

- domain entities, claims, relationships, and longitudinal source states;
- item-level evidence use, exclusion, conflict, dependence, and uncertainty;
- the decision owner, frame, constraints, recommendation, and override trace;
- the later action, reveal, outcome, and decision-quality regret;
- reusable lessons about which signals were reliable for which claim and
  decision family.

Private data may later reveal internal constraints, channel economics,
inventory, customer behavior, intended action, or outcomes that external
evidence cannot. It sharpens production judgment; it is not the default wedge.
Any retained derived learning depends on lawful and contractual permission.
If the decisive input is unavailable, Forseti holds.

Later Cleaning maturity can reduce analyst labor and improve entity resolution,
deduplication, normalization, contradiction handling, independence analysis,
and reproducibility at scale. Later Judgment maturity can reuse explicit traces,
compare decisions and outcomes, surface analogous evidence, calibrate action
ceilings, and increase throughput. Neither is a generic moat until repeated
decision and outcome evidence shows commercial effect.

## Beauty First, Then A Transfer Test

Beauty is the first application because its company decisions are exposed to
fast-moving, strategically biased and differently timed evidence across brands,
retailers, consumers, creators, claims, ingredients, channels and organizations,
and Forseti already has reusable beauty venues, evidence assets, cases, and
adjacent Aphrodite work.

Aphrodite Signals evidence can belong in the ultimate Forseti product when it is
decision-relevant. Ownership remains clean: Capture preserves source records;
Aphrodite owns its creator-signal product logic; Forseti consumes authorized
outputs as one evidence family and adjudicates them with non-creator evidence.
Aphrodite success alone does not validate Forseti's horizontal thesis.

Expansion beyond beauty is allowed only when the next category reuses material
ontology, longitudinal evidence structures, decision traces, outcome-learning
logic, buyer workflow, or distribution. Shared reviews, retailers, launches, or
source types alone are not a terminal bridge.

## Terminal Ambition And Economics Boundary

The ambition is an enterprise decision-intelligence company with economics that
could eventually be comparable to category leaders such as AlphaSense, not a
small research boutique. AlphaSense reported more than $600 million in ARR and
7,000+ enterprise customers on 2026-06-03
([company release](https://www.alpha-sense.com/press/alphasense-raises-350m-at-7-5b-valuation-and-surpasses-600m-in-annual-recurring-revenue/)).

That is a dated ambition comparator, not a TAM calculation or an argument that
Forseti can reach AlphaSense scale easily. Forseti has no validated WTP, buyer
count, distribution engine, or repeatable unit economics from which to support
such a forecast. Terminal ambition does not replace startability or proof.

## Explicit Non-Claims

This thesis is not:

- selection of the first beauty buyer, leading decision family, offer, price,
  TAM, SAM, SOM, competitor position, or GTM route;
- buyer validation, WTP proof, distribution proof, or repeatability proof;
- a claim of universal applicability or source-world completeness;
- proof that all public or entitled sources are currently captured or licensed;
- proof of mature Cleaning, autonomous Judgment, calibrated superiority,
  blind-judgment performance, or reliable forecasts;
- a dashboard, monitoring, data-feed, Amazon, transaction-data, or private-data
  integration recommendation;
- implementation, source acquisition, outreach, publishing, or Studio authority;
- proof that AlphaSense-scale revenue is reachable.
