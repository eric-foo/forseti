## Terminal Capability Hypothesis

Forseti's best reachable terminal hypothesis is an **institutional
evidence-adjudication and decision-learning layer for consequential decisions**.

The buyer does not buy "source weighting." The buyer buys a better decision:

```text
frame the decision and claims
-> acquire the relevant public and authorized private evidence
-> determine what each item can actually support
-> weight each item for this claim and decision
-> reconcile conflicts, dependence, missingness, and uncertainty
-> choose a right-sized action
-> preserve the decision trace
-> observe the reveal or outcome
-> learn which signals deserved weight in this decision family
```

Research, capture, claim-level traceability, action selection, and outcome
memory all matter. Decision-relative evidence weighting is the **epistemic
heart**: the central discipline for deciding what the available evidence lets
Forseti know and how strongly it justifies an action.

## The Epistemic Heart: Decision-Relative Evidence Weighting

"Epistemic" means concerned with how a claim is known, what justifies believing
it, and how confident the decision may be.

Evidence weight belongs to the triple:

```text
(evidence item, claim, decision frame)
```

It is never a permanent property of a source class. The same Reddit discussion
can be close to the phenomenon for a claim about organic conversation, useful
first-hand evidence for individual experience, weak evidence of market
prevalence, and remote testimony for scientific causation. Static ladders such
as "peer reviewed = high" or "Reddit = authentic = high" are therefore
prohibited. The controlling C2 doctrine owns the detailed merits axes and trace
behavior; this thesis adopts its core principle, not every proposed
implementation detail.

The important commercial effect is **marginal evidence sensitivity**. A small
but materially different item can tip a close decision when it changes the
weakest load-bearing basis, breaks an apparent tie, reveals dependence among
apparently independent sources, or changes the action ceiling. No fabricated
"5% weight" is required for that item to matter.

## AI Advantage — Adopted Hypothesis, Not Numeric-Precision Claim

Humans can and do weigh evidence and make strong judgments. Forseti may sell
accountable human-analyst judgment before an autonomous Judgment Spine is
calibrated.

AI's structural advantage hypothesis is narrower and stronger: AI makes it
economically feasible to maintain explicit, item-level, context-sensitive
weighting across far more heterogeneous evidence than a human can reliably hold
in working memory; to re-evaluate the same corpus when the claim or decision
frame changes; to preserve why each item was or was not load-bearing; and to
surface a marginal item that changes the decision.

Current Judgment doctrine remains qualitative. Forseti does not claim that an
AI knows an evidence item deserves exactly 5%, nor that a numerical weight is
real because a model emitted it. Numeric or ordinal weighting becomes
admissible only through a separately accepted, calibrated method with outcome
evidence and explicit uncertainty. Until then, false precision is a defect,
not sophistication.

## Human Judgment Now; Judgment Spine Later

The absence of a mature, outcome-calibrated Judgment Spine does not forbid
judgment products.

Allowed now, subject to the selected vertical's evidence and domain competence:

- human-adjudicated rankings, shortlists, recommendations, and action calls;
- explicit qualitative weighing of load-bearing and supporting evidence;
- scenario judgment, counter-cases, uncertainty, and named cruxes;
- mechanical momentum or acceleration measures plus human interpretation at
  the strength the observations support.

Not allowed without further evidence:

- claims of calibrated autonomous superiority;
- exact expected conversion, ROI, probability, spend, or rate without the
  required data and accepted model;
- causal commercial claims from public proxies alone;
- guaranteed forecasts or numeric evidence weights emitted without a validated
  calibration basis.

The mature Judgment Spine is the path from valuable but capacity-limited human
adjudication to repeatable, auditable, high-dimensional judgment with outcome
memory. Human analyst judgment is a legitimate commercial starting mode, not a
failure state.

## Public-Information Operating Assumption

Owner direction adopts a **public-first attainability presumption**: if
information is public, Forseti may treat it as eligible for discovery and may
pursue an authorized route to acquire it.

This is not a blanket claim that every public byte is currently or permanently
obtainable. Public sources can still be blocked, deleted, paywalled,
login-gated, geographically restricted, rate-limited, expensive, unstable, or
legally/contractually constrained. Source-specific access, rights, cost,
completeness, freshness, and durability gates remain owned by Scanning and Data
Capture.

The Commission Signal Board (CSB) may structure the information need, candidate
signals, counterevidence, and source-family plan. It does not retrieve evidence.
Scanning discovers and routes candidate sources; Capture owns retrieval and
preservation. No product lane may convert "public" into an unverified capture
or entitlement claim.

## What May Compound Into A Moat

Provenance is mandatory trust infrastructure and never the moat.

The terminal moat hypothesis is the accumulated, outcome-linked system of:

- decision-family-specific signal reliability priors;
- histories of which evidence was load-bearing and why;
- revealed failure modes, misses, counterfactuals, and decision-quality regret;
- customer workflow and authorized internal-evidence integration;
- longitudinal evidence that cannot be reconstructed later;
- reusable human/AI decision traces that improve later adjudication.

None of these moat claims is proven. The proposed signal-reliability ledger has
no real rows and the Judgment Spine has no Judgment-quality case history that
validates the terminal hypothesis.

compared after generation; their rankings and verdicts are not anchors.

## Corrected Candidate Selection Contract

Generate buyer problems where value depends on adjudicating heterogeneous,
conflicting, strategically biased, incomplete, or differently timed evidence.
For each serious candidate, test:

1. The cost of misweighting evidence and the decision owner who bears it.
2. Whether the relevant evidence is heterogeneous enough for adjudication to matter.
3. Whether Forseti can lawfully and durably acquire enough of the evidence.
4. Whether a human analyst can deliver valuable judgment now.
5. Whether AI can make the judgment materially broader, faster, more consistent, or more auditable.
6. Whether buyers can inspect convincing proof before trusting a consequential recommendation.
7. Decision frequency, urgency, outcome/reveal speed, and objective learning potential.
8. Existing expenditure, reachable budget, distribution difficulty, and regulatory exposure.
9. The path from expert service to reusable product without hiding permanent consulting economics.
10. Whether outcome-linked reliability memory can compound into a defensible advantage.

## Aphrodite Boundary

Aphrodite Signals is a concurrent vertical business candidate, not Forseti's
terminal company and not the anchor for the regenerated candidate universe. It
may commercialize human-adjudicated creator decisions under its own controlling
sources and proof gates. Its cases may later exercise Forseti's evidence-
weighting and outcome-learning machinery, but Aphrodite success would not by
itself validate the terminal horizontal thesis.

Aphrodite Studio is separately owner-ratified as an independent shared-data
revenue lane under the creator-signal charter. This thesis neither governs nor
reopens that lane and authorizes no Studio execution or client contact.
