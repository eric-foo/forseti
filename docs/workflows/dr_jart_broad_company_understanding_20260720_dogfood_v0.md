## Hypothesis

The existing company-intelligence contract can produce broad, Problem-Framing-
ready Understanding without a new schema or report phase when the commission:

1. binds the full existing eight-lens company model rather than a narrow
   proposition audit; and
2. requires at least one discriminating check for every material observable
   tension when a lawful, non-dominated route exists, otherwise a typed gap.

The run may identify observable tensions and candidate problem hypotheses. It
must not assign pain, severity, priority, business importance, cause, buyer,
ICP, urgency, demand or defect prevalence, recommendation, or response.

## Success And Failure

Success requires all eight existing company lenses to be answered, honestly
gapped, or inapplicable with rationale; each material tension to have a
reproducible evidence chain and one discriminating check or typed gap; required
routes and failures to be preserved; and the normal acquisition seal to pass.

The comparison with Marc Jacobs Beauty uses:

- breadth of relevant company-model coverage;
- directness and independence of load-bearing evidence;
- unresolved load-bearing gaps;
- reproducibility of tensions and their discriminating checks; and
- qualitative decision usefulness under the existing six outcome signals.

Raw source, review, or observation count is descriptive inventory only and
cannot establish success. Company maturity and source availability remain
material confounders.

Return `INCONCLUSIVE` if the acquisition blocks, the evidence world does not
surface a material tension, or the completed Deliver artifact is unavailable.
Return `REJECT` if the run crosses into Problem Framing, inflates an unsupported
prevalence or causal claim, or obtains apparent breadth through padding.

## Boundary

This dogfood changes no Intelligence Cycle, CSB, Scanning, Capture, validator,
schema, registry, scoring, monitoring, or latent-problem doctrine. A successful
run is product-learning evidence only; it may support later adversarial review
and owner adjudication, never automatic doctrine installation.

## Acquire & Seal Failure Diagnosis

CSB did trigger. The commission-stage board validly bound all eight routes and
ended at `COMMISSION_SEALED_PRE_SCAN` with `not_checked` rows. The failure
occurred after commissioning:

- Scanning used 8 screening moves and 12 exact queries, then recorded
  `capture_requests: 0` and `closeout_state: no_candidate_after_discovery`.
- One owned surface or one typed gap was often enough to make a lens appear
  covered. Administrative breadth substituted for evidence depth.
- When product-level Sephora review rows were unavailable, the run closed
  response deepening instead of finding another non-dominated way to test the
  underlying customer-experience question.
- Major tensions did not receive enough independent or discriminating evidence:
  two self-selected Reddit threads carried the response tension; one local
  retailer notice carried channel contraction; one comparator PDP carried
  competitive context; current independent trade remained sparse.
- The six outcome signals were declared in the commission and seal, but
  evidence foundation did not operate as a strong acquisition stopping test.
  A lens being touched, gapped, or preserved was treated too much like the
  evidence job being done.
- The later preservation recovery correctly reused only already observed URLs.
  It repaired provenance and enabled Deliver, but by design could not repair
  the thin evidence world.

Acquire & Seal should be the evidence-quality center of an Understanding phase.
Here it succeeded as a lifecycle gate only after recovery; it did not succeed
as the strongest analytical part of the cycle.

## Completeness Interpretation For The Next Dogfood

A company-intelligence **lens** is one of the eight existing domains:
positioning; offerings and claims; markets and channels; strategic and
operating moves; customer and community response; competitor and substitute
context; contradictions; and evidence gaps. A lens is a question domain, not a
minimum evidence allowance. `complete`, `gap`, or `not_applicable` describes
coverage accountability; it does not by itself prove evidence sufficiency.

The current learning supports an asymmetric operating stance:

- **Acquire & Seal is completeness-first.** For the bound question, push the
  evidence foundation toward the edge of diminishing returns: test
  load-bearing tensions with independent or discriminating evidence, exhaust
  or dominate plausible high-value routes, and name the material residuals
  accepted at seal. This is much closer to the owner-invoked Mini God Tier
  capability bar than to a minimal viable scan; MGT itself is a high-coverage
  target, not an 80/20 shortcut.
- **Smallest Complete still governs the intervention.** It prevents unrelated
  capture, speculative infrastructure, and repeated low-value sources. It must
  not be misread as permission to stop after the first source that touches a
  lens. The smallest complete evidence world may be large.
- **Deliver is compression-first.** Once the evidence world is sealed, use SCI
  aggressively: one governing frame, the fewest decision-useful conclusions,
  evidence and qualifications adjacent, audit detail behind the narrative,
  and no padding.

Full God Tier acquisition is not adopted by this finding. Without a
question-bound stopping rule it would invite unlimited source accumulation and
recurring cost. The candidate next-run bar is MGT-level evidence capability for
the bound Understanding question, with accepted residuals named at commission
and seal; owner adjudication is still required before this becomes standing
Intelligence Cycle doctrine.

## What Made Deliver Use The Evidence Better

Deliver improved usefulness through synthesis choices, not through additional
evidence:

1. **Fresh-context binding.** The actor started from the passing seal and bound
   question, avoiding acquisition chatter, route troubleshooting, and
   context-window drift.
2. **One governing company frame.** Cicapair/redness became the organizing
   company-system frame rather than a list of products or sources.
3. **Tension-first organization.** Formula continuity, item choice,
   geography/channel, visible price expression, and review measurement were
   separated into distinct tensions instead of being compressed into one vague
   performance problem.
4. **Claim-class discipline.** Observed fact, bounded inference, and unknown
   stayed separate; two attributable threads established existence only, never
   recurrence or prevalence.
5. **Decision adjacency.** Every major conclusion named the later question it
   could safely inform and the observable that would change the view, without
   silently performing Problem Framing.
6. **Evidence adjacency and compression.** Decisive evidence and its material
   qualification remained beside each claim. The report was shorter than Marc
   Jacobs despite its broader question.

These are real Deliver learnings, but they cannot compensate for inadequate
acquisition. Better synthesis of thin evidence is still thin intelligence.
