## Status And Purpose

Status: `OWNER_ACCEPTED_OPERATING_DIRECTION_PRE_DISCOVERY`.

On 2026-07-15, the owner accepted this sequence: build only enough private
method credibility to make discovery coherent; choose beauty companies as
observation subjects rather than presumed customers; investigate one company at
a time through a company-specific CSB and bounded deep scan; find or falsify a
recurring problem; then build wedge-specific authority and seek private buyer
confirmation before hyperspecializing GTM.

This is the current successor for beauty GTM problem discovery and target
selection. It replaces the former demand-signal GTM motion and consumer-demand
target-selection route as live guidance. Their dated facts remain historical;
their buyer, ranking, decision-family, and sequencing instructions are not
current. This contract does not authorize outreach, public positioning,
pricing, implementation, or a final wedge claim.

## Operating Target

Find a recurring, expensive beauty-company decision problem that Forseti can
adjudicate unusually well, then hyperspecialize the Decision Sprint around the
strongest verified problem-buyer-decision pattern.

```text
private method authority
-> company selection
-> one-company CSB and deep scan
-> company problem hypotheses
-> cross-company recurrence or falsification
-> private confirmation
-> accountable buyer and consequential decision
-> same-decision proof and wedge-specific authority
-> owner-selected GTM wedge
```

## Frozen Distinctions

- A studied company is not automatically a buyer, prospect, lead, or ICP.
- Public evidence may nominate a problem hypothesis; it cannot confirm pain,
  urgency, budget, willingness to pay, or a live buying decision.
- CSB decides what evidence to look for; Scanning tests venues; Capture preserves
  selected material. None qualifies buyers or clears a wedge.
- Retailer entry and every other beauty decision family remain non-ranked.
- Decision adjudication is the product. The Research Engine is the bounded
  discovery advantage, not the sold product.
- Authority content is an access enabler; attention or engagement is not pull.

## Thin Internal Method Authority

Private working statement:

> Forseti helps an accountable company owner make, narrow, sequence, delay,
> defend, or hold a consequential decision by discovering and reconciling the
> external evidence that can materially change the action. A bounded Decision
> Sprint fixes one Decision Frame, evidence cutoff, deadline, and private-context
> boundary; returns a decision memo, evidence appendix, evidence-world/gap note,
> decision trace, uncertainty, and reversal conditions; and holds when a
> decisive input is missing.

This may establish method clarity, inspectability, evidence discipline, and a
consistent internal frame. It must not claim a selected problem, buyer, wedge,
offer, price, buyer validation, willingness to pay, repeatability, mature
Cleaning, autonomous Judgment, calibrated superiority, or broad monitoring.

Do not begin a broad beauty newsletter, generic LinkedIn authority campaign,
content calendar, public pain monitor, dashboard, or "AI for beauty decisions"
positioning before discovery earns a specific problem and buyer.

## Company Universe And Mix

Do not choose indie/DTC or large companies as the whole universe before the
problem is known. Use observable operating footprint rather than unverified
revenue bands. The initial eight-company allocation is:

| Observable footprint | Target | Discovery value | Main risk |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Independent, DTC-led, or early multichannel | 2 | Reachable owners, fast decisions, visible experiments | Founder-specific problems, weak budgets, idiosyncrasy |
| Growth or mid-market multichannel | 4 | Best balance of material stakes, public evidence, professional ownership, plausible budget, and access | Private context and boundaries still vary |
| Scaled portfolio or business unit | 2 | Large consequences, rich evidence, contrast, and transfer test | Slow access, diffuse ownership, decisive internal data |

Growth/mid-market multichannel is the discovery center of gravity, not the
preselected ICP. If one problem recurs only in a coherent footprint, that
condition becomes evidence for the later wedge.

## Company Selection

Create one run-local pool of about 20 companies from at least three independent
company-universe sources. No single retailer, publication, founder network, or
"hot brand" list may define it. Deduplicate company and brand-parent identities.
The pool is temporary research scaffolding, not a CRM, registry, lead list,
market map, ICP, or monitor.

A company is eligible only when it:

1. actively commercializes owned beauty products;
2. has a current observable operating footprint;
3. exposes at least three lawfully accessible, independent evidence families;
4. has a consequential action or later reveal that could become observable;
5. is not obviously controlled entirely by inaccessible private information;
6. adds material category, footprint, channel, or context contrast.

Record only company identity, category, footprint, evidence-family availability,
dated activity without causal interpretation, later-reveal route, fatal evidence
boundary, and contrast value. Do not record a presumed problem, buyer, or wedge.

Prefer evidence visibility, observable actions/reveals, contrast, and fewer
fatal boundaries. Use access only as a tiebreaker. Do not prefer fame, apparent
distress, founder leadership, easy contact, a named retailer, or familiarity.

Predeclare three seeds: one independent/DTC-led company, one growth/mid-market
multichannel company, and one scaled portfolio or business unit, with different
beauty categories where feasible. After all three close, choose later companies
to falsify or discriminate emerging hypotheses. Include at least two negative
or apparently stable cases and stop by eight deep company investigations.

## One-Company Research Cycle

Complete and close each company before starting the next:

1. **Company-specific CSB:** bind one exact company and the context "discover
   evidence that may nominate pressure around a consequential beauty decision,
   without ranking a decision family." CSB emits no pain, buyer, or wedge verdict.
2. **Bounded Scanning:** run the default broad scout and targeted deepening;
   collect contradictions and negatives as deliberately as supporting evidence.
3. **Selective Capture:** preserve only material observations, contradictions,
   negatives, and later-reveal anchors through existing authorized routes.
4. **Company problem map:** close with observed facts, contradictions, no more
   than three hypotheses, possible consequential action, unverified accountable
   role, decisive unknown, disqualifier, confirmation need, and retain/hold/kill.

Default per-company caps are six source families, 20 unique decision-relevant
items, a 180-day current window, historical context up to 24 months only when it
changes interpretation, and a stop when new sources stop changing the picture
or a hard access wall controls the answer. These caps do not prove sufficiency.

## Recurrence, Confirmation, And Wedge Gate

A recurring problem hypothesis requires the same underlying decision pressure
across at least three independent companies. Similar surface signals do not
count when decisions, causes, or consequences differ. After the three seeds,
select the next company for falsification or discrimination, not convenient
confirmation. If multiple patterns remain inseparable, return the smallest
discriminating company or evidence request.

```text
observation
-> problem hypothesis
-> recurring problem hypothesis
-> privately confirmed decision pressure
-> accountable buyer and consequential decision
-> commercially plausible pattern
-> candidate Decision Sprint wedge
```

Private confirmation must bind action, accountable owner, decision window,
consequence, current substitute and cost, decisive unknown, and minimum private
context. A candidate wedge also needs lawful evidence, bounded private context,
reachable owners, plausible substitute spend, bounded analyst delivery, a
faithful same-decision proof path, and bounded liability. A failed hard gate
cannot be offset by signal volume, authority engagement, market size, or scoring.

## Authority And GTM Sequence

| Authority layer | Earliest legitimate evidence | Current state |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Method authority | Inspectable method, uncertainty, evidence discipline, and holds | May be built privately now |
| Decision-family authority | Faithful same-decision case and repeated problem-specific evidence | Blocked until recurrence |
| Commercial authority | Qualified owner use, budget-adjacent behavior, commitment, or payment | Not proven |

After recurrence, build one wedge-specific same-decision case before narrow
authority content or separately authorized buyer confirmation. Broad public
positioning, offer design, pricing, outreach, and distribution stay blocked
until the problem, accountable buyer, and decision family are evidence-backed
and owner-selected.

## Stop Rules And Existing Artifacts

- Synthesize after three seeds; do not select a wedge.
- Stop by eight companies, earlier saturation, or a decisive access wall.
- Return `NO_DIRECTION_CLEARS` if no coherent recurring problem survives.
- Hold any surviving public pattern at hypothesis until private confirmation.
- The Forseti owner selects the final wedge.

The superseded demand-signal GTM design and consumer-demand target-selection
brief remain historical evidence only. The suspended offer hypothesis and buyer
proof packet are different lifecycle roles: this contract does not supersede
them, and they may be rebound only after a wedge is selected.
