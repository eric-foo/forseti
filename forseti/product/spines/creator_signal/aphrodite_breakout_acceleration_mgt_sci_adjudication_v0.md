## MGT Target

Mini God Tier target: capture most of the practical value of "spot the next
breakout creator early enough to inspect or sponsor" without building full
growth-intelligence infrastructure.

Under SCI, the bounded version is:

```text
internal_not_validated_acceleration_watchlist
```

It is an internal triage surface or validation run only. It may sort creators for
human inspection, but it must not itself authorize spend, outreach, lead export,
or an automated buyer-facing score. Under the 2026-07-12 amendment, an
accountable human analyst may consume the evidence and make the bounded
buyer-facing forecast defined above.

The MGT edge is not a bigger formula. It is the combination of:

- own-baseline movement, not raw popularity;
- no acceleration language before enough comparable history exists;
- fit-relevance guard so irrelevant virality does not look sponsorable;
- paid/anomaly posture that downgrades or withholds instead of accusing;
- sponsorability overlay kept separate from acceleration;
- held-out proof that acceleration adds lift beyond momentum;
- accepted residuals named up front.

## Adjudication of the ChatGPT Pro Memo

Accepted:

- `GO_VALIDATION_GATED_INTERNAL_WATCHLIST` is the right posture.
- Acceleration is different from momentum only if it adds predictive lift among
  creators with similar current movement.
- Two comparable observations can support velocity/delta only; acceleration
  needs at least three comparable windows or enough per-content observations to
  compare slopes.
- False-positive handling should use posture language such as
  `anomaly_risk`, `paid_distribution_possible`, `downgrade`, and `withhold`, not
  accusations.
- Sponsor-earlier economics belong in the validation spec. The signal's first
  job is buying earlier attention, not authorizing sponsorship spend.
- Held-out validation must compare against raw popularity, raw momentum,
  posting cadence, random eligible creators, and a manual recent-high-view
  heuristic.

Accepted with modification:

- The memo's numeric examples, such as 3x base rate, 1.25x best baseline, or
  5-10%/10-20% precision bands, are useful calibration examples only. They are
  not doctrine. The sealed validation spec must bind K, cost tier, base rate,
  and pass/fail thresholds from the actual scouting decision.
- "Manipulation-risk screens" are not a detector family. They are downgrade and
  withhold rules inside the acceleration posture. Public evidence can make a
  signal less safe to rank; it usually cannot prove botting, fake engagement, or
  paid manipulation.

Rejected:

- Any route that builds a general anomaly-detection platform before the
  acceleration lift assumption is proven.
- Any route that merges acceleration, ad-load, ad-reception, and fit into one
  customer-facing score.
- Any route that treats a promising watchlist as a sponsorship execution system.
- Any route that creates a precomputed metric family before field-level posture,
  reason, coverage, and rebuildability contracts are bound.
- Any route that converts the memo into a broad ML, dashboard, or runtime plan.

## Smallest Complete Design Target

The next design target is a sealed validation spec, not a build.

The spec should bind:

| Field | SCI-bound requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Candidate universe | Under-radar fragrance creators only; no public person-level directory behavior. |
| Inputs | Source-visible creator/content metrics with lineage, freshness, sample support, and compatible windows. |
| Minimum posture | One point = withhold; two comparable points = velocity only; three comparable windows = weak acceleration eligible. |
| Feature family | Robust own-baseline relative movement: age-normalized velocity, relative lift, fast/slow crossover, repeated fit-relevant overperformance, and top-post concentration guard. |
| False-positive posture | Paid/gifted/affiliate markers, top-post concentration, off-topic virality, giveaway/controversy language, engagement-quality anomalies, freshness, and compatibility can downgrade/withhold only. |
| Sponsorability | Separate overlay: low/moderate ad-load plus sponsored-vs-organic performance posture. Never part of acceleration itself. |
| Baselines | Raw popularity, raw momentum/velocity, recent cadence, random eligible creators, manual recent-high-view heuristic. |
| Outputs | Internal banded watchlist with receipts, limitations, and withhold reasons. No buyer-facing score. |
| Success | Incremental held-out lift over momentum and simple heuristics at the K/cost tier actually used. |
| Failure | No incremental lift, lift dominated by one-post/off-topic/paid-looking spikes, or too little eligible history to populate useful rows. |
